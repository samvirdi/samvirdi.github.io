First Year Engineering Client Project
The CSPro

We had a client come in to our class and present a problem that they were facing in their daily lives, and we had to offer a solution for it. Our client had severe cerebral palsy which caused her to not be able to select the clothes she wants to wear from her closet. We decided to make a contraption that would be able to connected to ,and be maneuvered by her wheelchair. The picture attached was the final product!
